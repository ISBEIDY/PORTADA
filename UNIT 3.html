UNIT 3 PAST PROGRESSIVE



El pasado progresivo pone énfasis en el curso de una acción en el pasado.
Al verbo se le agrega la terminacion (ING) .
EXAMPLE: TO BE in simple past + Verb + ING.

FORM
 ____________________________________________________________________________
| SUBJECT      | POSITIVE          | NEGATIVE            | QUESTION          |
|______________|___________________|_____________________|___________________|
| I/HE/SHE/IT  | I was speaking.   | I wasn't speaking.  | Was I speaking?   |
|______________|___________________|_____________________|___________________|
| YOU/WE/THEY  | We were speaking. | We weren't speaking.| Were We speaking? |
|______________|___________________|_____________________|___________________|


EXAMPLES IN POSITIVE

-They were standing (stand) on the corner when I saw them.
-The sun was shining (shine) when I woke up this morning.
-It was raining (rain) when I left the office.
-She got very sick while she was traveling (travel) overseas.
-They were speeding (speed) when the accident happened.

EXAMPLES IN NEGATIVE

-They weren't standing (stand) on the corner when I saw them.
-The sun wasn't shining (shine) when I woke up this morning.
-It wasn't raining (rain) when I left the office.
-She got very sick while she wasn't traveling (travel) overseas.
-They weren't speeding (speed) when the accident happened.

EXAMPLES IN QUESTION

-Were They standing (stand) on the corner when I saw them?
-Was the sun shining (shine) when I woke up this morning?
-Was It raining (rain) when I left the office?
-Was she traveling (travel) overseas?
-Were They speeding (speed) when the accident happened?
